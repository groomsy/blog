What is it?

Scenic is an application that allows you to find what's around you. The application uses your device's current location to find venues that are near you. You will then be presented with a collection of photos that have been taken near your current location. See an interesting picture? Quickly pull up walking directions to the venue where the image was taken. Don't want to give Scenic permission to use your device's Location Services permission? No problem. Once you decline to give Scenic permission to use Location Services, you can use Scenic's search feature to search for a location and pull up photos near the queried location.

Scenic is backed by Foursquare and Instagram. Find a photo that you like? You can like it through your Instagram account. Are you currently at the place? Use the Check in on Foursquare button to quickly launch Foursquare.


New in Version 1.1
